About Me

Bilingual. Operational.
Self-taught.

I'm Mohammad, based in Amman, Jordan. My career started on the floor of restaurants and hotel lobbies, where I learned that the difference between a good experience and a great one is almost always in the details nobody else bothered to notice.

Along the way I taught myself to code. Not to become a developer, but because I kept running into problems that software could solve and nobody had built the solution yet. So I built it myself. Those tools are now used daily by real teams.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Hotel Management from Yarmouk University, graduating with Excellent distinction. I'm fully bilingual in Arabic and English, certified in food safety by the Jordan Food & Drug Administration, and I work best in environments where the bar is high and the work actually matters.

What drives me

I can't walk past a broken process. Not because I was trained to fix things, but because leaving something worse than I found it has never felt like an option.

How I work

Calm under pressure, thorough with details, and direct about problems. I'd rather raise an issue early than pretend it isn't there.

What I'm looking for

A role where showing initiative is rewarded, not just tolerated. Somewhere I can grow, contribute, and actually make a difference in how things run.
